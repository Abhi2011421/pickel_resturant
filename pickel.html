Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master chef Alexander Pickel, our restaurant has been serving gourmet dishes with a personal touch for over a decade. What started as a small bistro has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

We believe in sourcing the freshest local ingredients and combining them with international flavors to create dishes that surprise and delight. Our menu changes seasonally to reflect the best produce available.

At Pickel, dining is more than just a meal—it's an experience. From our carefully curated wine list to our attentive yet unobtrusive service, every detail is designed to create memorable moments.
